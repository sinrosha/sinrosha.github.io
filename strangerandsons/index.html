The tale of Stranger & Sons

On a moonlit night, deep in the jungles of the Western Ghats in India, there lived a mythical two tailed, three eyed creature who chanced upon juniper berries hidden in the secret pocket of a sari blouse. The story of this gin began with her.

She’s known to appear on full moon nights holding a pot with her human hand and hiding secret ingredients in her two tails. Some say you can still see her on a very still night. Her pug marks disappear as soon as she makes them, almost like there’s an invisible wind who’s sole responsibility is keeping her existence a mystery for all eyes; except the ones she chooses. And even then, she always uses her third eye to connect with them.
